The Core Principle

One entity.
Any model.

AgentOS is model-agnostic by design. Your entity — its knowledge, its behavior, its configuration — is completely separate from the model powering it. Swap the model and nothing breaks.

Start with a cloud model for speed. Switch to local when your data is sensitive. Move to MoE when your domain demands deeper reasoning. The entity doesn't care. It just keeps working.

Every model swap happens from your dashboard. No engineering. No downtime. No rebuilding anything.

Cloud Models

Maximum capability. On demand.

Cloud models give your entity access to the most powerful language models available — 70B+ parameters, massive context windows, and the ability to scale to any volume without managing infrastructure.

Best when: you need maximum reasoning depth, your data isn't privacy-sensitive, or your entity handles complex multi-step queries at scale.

02
Local Models

Your data never leaves.

Local models run inference entirely on your machine. No data is sent to external services. No API calls. No egress. Your conversations, your queries, your records — private by architecture.

Best when: your entity handles sensitive business data, compliance requirements restrict cloud processing, or you want zero marginal cost on inference.

03
Mixture of Experts

Not one model. The right model for every question.

A standard model handles every query the same way. A Mixture of Experts framework routes each query to the specialist model best equipped to answer it — producing deeper, more accurate responses without the overhead of running a single massive model on everything.

Best when: your entity spans multiple knowledge areas, query types vary significantly, or you're running a large-scale Wide oracle that needs deep accuracy across a broad surface.

MoE is the default recommendation for Eve/Wide entities running on Prism.
