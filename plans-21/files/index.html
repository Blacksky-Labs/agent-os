What Is An Entity

Every entity is an expert oracle on its domain.

An AgentOS entity isn't a chatbot. It's a dedicated intelligence built deep on a specific domain — your business, your industry, your data.

Every entity comes in two archetypes. Focused entities go narrow and deep — built on your data, your records, your internal context. Wide entities cast a broad net — built on large external knowledge bases, real-time feeds, and datasets at scale.

Same platform. Same architecture. Different defaults. Fully configurable.
