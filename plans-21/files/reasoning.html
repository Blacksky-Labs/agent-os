You build the jobs from your dashboard. You choose what runs, when it runs, and what data it runs on. The entity executes. You wake up to answers.

04
Identity Layer

Your entity knows you before you introduce yourself.

A soft login tracks users via cookies and local storage the moment they arrive — no account required. Your entity starts building a profile immediately. When they eventually connect via Google or create an account, every prior interaction merges into their authenticated record seamlessly.

No friction. No forced sign-ups. No lost history. The entity always offers the option to connect — and always works without it.

Your entity takes calls.

AgentOS entities aren't chat-only. They handle phone calls — and every call feeds the same reasoning layer. Transcripts analyzed in real time. Caller matched to existing profile by number. Sentiment scored. Budget, timeline, and intent extracted. CRM updated before the call ends.

A customer who calls Monday and chats Thursday is one person to your entity. One profile. One picture. One history.
